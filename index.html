Of Gods and Slaves
Of Gods and Slaves is a 2.5D isometric tactical RPG built in the Unity engine. It is being developed by solo-dev Studio PZD and is currently in Alpha status.

In OGaS, the player becomes the Controller, the God of Time, and will guide the Clone Legion I, led by clone-slave ROME-0 DAN:TES, as he battles for his freedom in a futuristic neo-Roman civilization. It is meant as a true spiritual successor to FFT, featuring satisfying spectacle and crunch in the combat, verticality and grandeur in the maps, and complex themes and dialogue in the narrative.
